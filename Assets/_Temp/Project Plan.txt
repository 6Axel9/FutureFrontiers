- Gameplay Format

- Initial Setup
	[Scan Arena Anchor]
	[Select Arena]
	[Confirm Selections]
- Units Setup
	[Scan Unit]
	[Confirm Unit]
	[Repeat Process]
- Startup Phase
	[Confirm Setup]
	[Wait For Response]
- Match Phase
	[Select Unit]
	[Select Action]
	[Confirm Action]
	[Repeat Process]
	[Pass Phase]
	[Wait For Response]
